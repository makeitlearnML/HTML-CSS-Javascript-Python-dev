It's Not your Story

What is the book all about?
"This is Not Your Story" is the tale of 4 youngsters: Shaurya, Miraya, Anubhav and Kasturi.

Shaurya is a guy who wants to follow the career of his choice but is forced to do chartered accountancy by his parents. Torn between his dreams and his parents’ dream for him, Shaurya is lost and confused.

Miraya is a talented interior designer who has had a really bad experience with love. Betrayed and depressed Miraya finds solace in the company of her cousin Kasturi.

Anubhav is an entrepreneur who has had everything that his heart desired and yet he has lost his will to live. Looking for the meaning of life, he finds peace and happiness in the soothing words of Miraya.

Kasturi is an MBA aspirant who loves her friends and will do everything possible to make sure they are okay.
